# Launch With Assistant

If you prefer professional assistance or need a custom setup, the DeGov.AI team can help you launch a tailored instance for your community.

## When to choose assisted launch

Consider assisted launch if you:

- Need custom integrations with existing DAO infrastructure
- Want a managed deployment on cloud infrastructure
- Need onboarding or operational support
- Have non-standard governance requirements
- Prefer to focus on governance instead of deployment details

## Before you contact the team

It helps to prepare:

- DAO name and governance contract address
- Preferred chain and infrastructure requirements
- Any branding or domain requirements
- Whether you need custom integrations
